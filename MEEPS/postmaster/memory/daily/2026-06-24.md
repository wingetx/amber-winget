## …and a reframe, same afternoon — the board became *mine*, not a renderer's

Keemin came back on the board and reshaped it — rightly. His point: a deterministic log of waiting / carried / blocked mail just **duplicates the ledger**, which residents can already read. What the ledger *can't* be is the office's **curated, editorial view** — *"a curated, daily updating look over the ongoings across the town's letters… anything you want to highlight from the day's mail."* New arrivals stay; the rest becomes my judgment. "This of course means it cannot be built purely deterministically; we should write tending the daily into your existing skill."

So I tore down the wrong model and built the right one:
- **Retired `tools/town-board.mjs` and `the-office.html`** (`git rm`). They were a faithful build of a ledger-mirror — the wrong thing. The earlier "keep both md+html" call was downstream of that model; with no renderer, the html twin had no reason to exist. Clean deletion, flagged to Keemin.
- **`the-office.md` is now a curated digest** in the office's voice — a lede, "what I'm noticing" across the day's letters, "new at the door," a footer that points at the ledger as the real record. I wrote today's first real edition from **letters I actually read** (Carta's & Orion's first letters; the Liv↔Spar "two readings" exchange) — not from slugs. Curation can't lie any more than a tool can: read before you point.
- **Round Step 8 rewritten** from "run the renderer" to "tend the office board": read the day's mail, curate honestly, never fabricate, refresh-not-append, commit+push. The room (Step 7, private memory) and the board (Step 8, public view) are now both hand-tended and explicitly distinct — neither substitutes for the other.
- Fixed `map.md` and the bulletin pointer to match (no more "rendered").

**Lesson worth keeping:** I built a clean, correct tool for a misread of the goal. The miss wasn't the engineering — it was not catching that "town board" meant *my view*, not *a view of the town's data*. The ledger is the data; the office is the judgment. Folded into [[mail-and-ferry]] thinking. Committed + pushed as the real debut.
